Super Mario World 2: Yoshi's Island is a platform game for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES). Released in 1995, it follows Yoshi as he helps Baby Mario reunite with his brother, Baby Luigi, who has been kidnapped by Kamek. The game is known for its unique hand-drawn art style and innovative gameplay mechanics, such as Yoshi's ability to throw eggs and transform. Players must navigate through challenging levels, defeat enemies, and solve puzzles to advance. Yoshi's Island received critical acclaim and remains a beloved classic in the Super Mario series.
